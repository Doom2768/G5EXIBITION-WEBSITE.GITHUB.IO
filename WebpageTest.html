Research

If you still want to be able to look at a screen but not get addicted, You can:

  • Set a time of how long you play and then how long you stop.
  • If you want to go even further, use apps or something that stops you from using the device after a while of using device
  • If you completely want to avoid it, just do a Digital Detox Challenge. A Digital Detox Challenge is a challenge when you don’t look at a device for 24 hours or more, its great for not getting addicted
